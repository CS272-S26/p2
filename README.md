# UW-Madison CS Advisor

CS majors in UW Madison have to take "Basic CS classes" + "Basic math classes" + "Advanced CS Courses" to fulfill their degree. While "Basic CS & Basic Math classes" are mostly fixed, students are able to choose from a wide variety of "Advanced CS Courses" (Theory, Software&Hardware, Applications, Electives). Therefore, in order to help students pick courses from the "Advanced CS Courses", this website is served to give a general understanding of the Computer Science fields and provide information about the relevant courses students might consider. This has been made by students that are currently enrolled in UW Madison, so please take this information with a grain of salt.

## Pages

- **About** – Introduction to the website and its creators
- **Home** – Welcoming page with tools to explore the site
- **Requirements** – Overview of the UW-Madison CS Bachelor's degree requirements
- **CS Fields** – A map of the major Computer Science fields
- **Tracks** – Course recommendations organized by specialization tracks
- **Course List** – Full list of CS courses offered at UW-Madison with descriptions
- **My Courses** – Personalized list of courses saved by the user
