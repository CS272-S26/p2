Introduction

Purpose: CS majors in UW Madison have to take "Basic CS classes" + "Basic math classes" + "Advanced CS Courses" to fulfill their degree. While "Basic CS & Basic Math classes" are mostly fixed, students are able to choose from a wide variety of "Advanced CS Courses" (Theory, Software&Hardware, Applications, Electives). Therefore, in order to help students pick courses from the "Advanced CS Courses" this website is served to give a general understanding of the Computer Science fields and provide information about the relevant courses students might consider.

Page Guide: The website will have the following pages.
Home: Welcoming page, there are some cool tools you can play around with.
Requirements: Description of current requirements for the UW Madison Bachelor's CS degree.
CS Fields: A map of the Computer Science Fields.
Course List: The CS courses that UW Madison offers.
Tracks: Course recommendations for specifc tracks.
