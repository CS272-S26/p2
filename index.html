Introduction

Purpose: CS majors in UW Madison have to take "Basic CS classes" + "Basic math classes" + "Advanced CS Courses" to fulfill their degree. While "Basic CS & Basic Math classes" are mostly fixed, students are able to choose from a wide variety of "Advanced CS Courses" (Theory, Software&Hardware, Applications, Electives). Therefore, in order to help students pick courses from the "Advanced CS Courses", this website is served to give a general understanding of the Computer Science fields and provide information about the relevant courses students might consider. This has been made by students that are currently enrolled in UW Madison, so please take this information with a grain of salt.

Page Guide: The website will have the following pages.
Home: Welcoming page, there are some cool tools you can play around with.
Requirements: Description of current requirements for the UW Madison Bachelor's CS degree.
CS Fields: A map of the Computer Science Fields.
Tracks: Course recommendations for specifc tracks.
Course List: The CS courses that UW Madison offers.
My Courses: The courses that a student selected as a favorite from the Course List page.
